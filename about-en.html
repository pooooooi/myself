About Self Map

Self Map is a private note for sorting thoughts and turning reflection into one small action.

Purpose

Thinking is useful, but when thoughts become too large, action becomes far away. Self Map helps you return from reflection to a small action.

What matters

  • No login required
  • Data is saved on the device
  • The app screen stays free from ads
  • Small actions matter more than big resolutions
