Small Action

Make the action small before waiting for motivation

A big action creates a heavy feeling before you start. A tiny action gives you a way back.

Make it almost too small

  • Create a document -> open the file
  • Reply -> open the recipient
  • Clean up -> move one thing nearby
  • Exercise -> stand and stretch for ten seconds
Ad space

The goal is returning

You do not need to finish everything. The important skill is returning in a small way after stopping.
