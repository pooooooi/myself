Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

1. About this app

Self Map is a note app for organizing reflection and turning it into a small action.

2. Data collection

Self Map does not send the text you enter to an external server. Your cards, scene names, connections, reflections, and language setting are saved on your device.

3. Use of data

The data is used to restore your notes, keep your map structure, show reflections, and remember the display language.

4. Sharing

Self Map does not share your entered data with third parties.

5. Storage

Because data is saved locally, it may be lost if you delete the app, clear browser data, reset the device, or lose the device. Use export for important notes.
